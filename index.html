The average H1B in tech pays $86,164/year.

Since 2012 the tech industry has sponsored 4,998 H1B work visas. Most of them paid $65,415 to $113,658 per year. The best city for an H1B is Jersey City, NJ with an average salary $38,564 above the local household median. Median household income is a good proxy for cost of living in an area.
